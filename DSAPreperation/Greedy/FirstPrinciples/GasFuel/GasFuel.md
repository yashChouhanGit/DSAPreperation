The Scenario

You are developing a feature for a navigation app. A car is traveling along a one-way road.

You are given an array gas, where gas[i] is the amount of fuel available at that station.

Each unit of fuel allows you to travel exactly one station forward.

You start at index 0 with 0 units of fuel (but you immediately collect the fuel at index 0).

The Goal:
Determine if the car can reach the last station in the array.

Example:
gas = [1, 0, 2, 0, 1]
